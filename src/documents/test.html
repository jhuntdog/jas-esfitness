Volunteer Opportunities

Through clinical trials, The Center for Pharmaceutical Research and our community volunteers are truly advancing medicine and changing lives.

CPR conducts clinical trials in virtually all medical specialties. Whether you are looking for a novel treatment for a specific condition, or are interested in healthy volunteer or vaccine trials, CPR offers volunteers a full range of clinical trial opportunities.

Experience and Expertise

Our Medical Director and Principal Investigator, John E. Ervin, M.D., has conducted over 500 clinical trials since founding CPR in 1986 and continues to offer safe, ethical treatment options to the Kansas City community.
